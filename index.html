Natal - RN, Brasil

Natal, the capital of Rio Grande do Norte in Brazil, is known as the "City of the Sun." It boasts stunning natural beauty, including its famous beaches. Ponta Negra is the most renowned beach, home to the iconic Morro do Careca (Bald Hill). Along the coastline, you'll find beach bars, restaurants, and shops. Beyond beaches, Natal offers cultural richness, gastronomy, and historical charm.🌴🌞🌴
